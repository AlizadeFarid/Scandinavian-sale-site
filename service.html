Customer Service

Our passion for quality doesn’t stop with our products. We think shipping should be blazing fast, returns should be hassle-free and getting in touch with us should be as easy as pie.

Read about our fair conditions on this page or follow the links to get to the nitty gritty details.

Shipping & Delivery

All orders placed before 2 p.m. Mon-Fri will we shipped the same day. We ship worldwide and offer both free standard shipping and express shipping if you’re in a hurry. Read more here.

Returns

Returns are free. Fill in the return form and return your unused goods putting the prepaid self-adhesive return label on the parcel that your order arrived in. We’ll return your money when the goods has been returned to our warehouse. Read our step-by-step instructions.
